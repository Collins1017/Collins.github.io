Common Exercises

The following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily routine.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body exercise to increase your strngth and endurane. Begin in a standin position, drop into a squat, and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quickly, and then jump up from a squatted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strength. To perform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor in a push up position then bring one knee up to your chest and then switch as quickly as you can (as through you are climbing a mountain).
